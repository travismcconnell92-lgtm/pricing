Fee Policies

What You Should Know

💳

Payment in Advance

Ready Legal collects fees in advance to ensure seamless legal service. For hourly work, we require an advance fee deposit (often incorrectly referred to as a "retainer"). The amount typically correlates with the scope of the project or, for longer matters such as litigation, an estimate covering an interim billing period. For flat-fee services, the full fee must be paid before work begins.

🚫

No Contingency Fees

Ready Legal does not operate on a contingency fee model. All legal services are billed according to the hourly rates or agreed-upon flat fees described above. If you need a personal injury attorney who operates on contingency, we would be happy to offer a referral.

🤝

Engagement Agreement Required

No legal services are performed until a written engagement agreement has been signed by both parties. Submitting a form or contacting the firm does not create an attorney-client relationship or obligate the firm to perform any services.

💬

Questions About Fees?

If you have any questions about our pricing or would like an estimate for your specific matter, contact Ready Legal. We are happy to discuss your case and provide clarity on potential costs before you commit to anything.
